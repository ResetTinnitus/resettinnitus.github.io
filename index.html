RESET TINNITUS

Reset Tinnitus App iOS

Our app is designed to generate a set of frequencies based on your tinnitus to create a acoustic coordinated reset neuromodulation generator.

The main generator of our app is the acoustic coordinated reset neuromodulation and helps tinnitus sufferers approach tinnitus habituation from a different angle. We suggest that an individual to not use the acoustic coordinated reset neuromodulation generator for more than three hours a day.

To help cope with this short amount of time, we have also included a natural noise generator to create sounds such as a heart beat, rain, fans, etc... and a white noise generator. We understand how frustrating tinnitus can be and we ebelive our app will help you calm your nerves and bring your focus back on your daily activities.


We recommend headphones while using our app. Our app has not be medically approved. Please consult your doctor.
