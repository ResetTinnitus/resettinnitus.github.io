Reset Tinnitus App iOS

Our app is designed to generate a set of frequencies based on your tinnitus to create a acoustic coordinated reset neuromodulation generator.

We drew inspiration from the Research Drawn in this paper

The main generator of our app is the acoustic coordinated reset neuromodulation and helps tinnitus sufferers approach tinnitus habituation from a different angle.


We recommend headphones while using our app. Our app has not be medically approved. Please consult your doctor.
